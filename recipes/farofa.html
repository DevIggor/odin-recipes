Farofa

  • 1 cup of cassava flour
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: chopped parsley or bacon bits for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until they are soft and fragrant.
  3. Gradually add the cassava flour, stirring constantly to prevent lumps.
  4. Continue to cook the mixture, stirring frequently, until the farofa is golden brown and crispy.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Remove from heat and transfer to a serving dish.
  7. Garnish with chopped parsley or bacon bits if desired.
