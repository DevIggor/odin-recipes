Lasagna

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 jar of marinara sauce
  • 2 cups of ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package directions.
  2. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef and drain the excess fat.
  3. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until they are soft and fragrant.
  4. Stir in the marinara sauce and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  6. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  7. Layer half of the cooked noodles, then half of the ricotta cheese, then half of the mozzarella cheese.
  8. Repeat the layers, ending with a layer of noodles on top.
  9. Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese over the top layer.
  10. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
