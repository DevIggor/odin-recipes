Pão de queijo

  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 cups of tapioca flour
  • 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 beaten eggs

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a saucepan, combine the milk, vegetable oil, and salt. Heat until the mixture is hot but not boiling.
  3. Remove from heat and gradually add the tapioca flour, stirring until smooth.
  4. Allow the mixture to cool slightly, then stir in the grated Parmesan cheese and beaten eggs until well combined.
  5. Using your hands, shape the dough into small balls and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the pão de queijo are puffed and golden brown.
